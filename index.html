Welcome to my Website!

My name is Yasmina, and here's just a little bit about me.

I was born & raised in Toronto, Ontario.

All my life I've loved the city life, so coming to London, Ontario to study Interactive Media Design was at first intimidating, but I soon learned that I would adore my program to the fullest.

I graduated from Ecole Secondaire Etienne-Brule.

I really enjoyed my time there, as we were less than 100 students in my grade. It enabled me to create really close bonds with everyone, and it really was a sad day when we all graduated and parted seperate ways.

I got to experience classic university experiences like living in rez, and foco.

Going from a small highschool of 700 students ranging from grade 7 to grade 12, we can all get an idea on how much of a shock factor the 31,000 undergrad students that surrounded me were. I felt lost in a sea of students all experiencing the same things as I was, but soon I grew accustomed to it and really loved the immense diversity within its campus.

I have a lot of talented friends in photography and design.

One thing that's great about living in Toronto, is that there is a plethora of artistic talent, some whose passions revolve around capturing great scenic photography, or even visual arts pieces. The culture and vibrancy of Toronto is something I miss, but I wouldn't have it any other way than at Western & Fanshawe.
